Our mission

Our mission is to help out people who have lost their jobs due to the pandemic and small business owners affected by the decrease in traffic. These people, Hyree, can do quick jobs (1-10) hours in companies that experience a sudden unexpected surge in demand, Hyrer. Our mission adds value to the Hyrer as they can ensure their customers do not encounter any delays. At the same time it benefits the Hyree who have lost their jobs.
